### Performance Plan

- **Goal/SLIs**: latency/throughput/error budgets
- **Current State**: symptoms, metrics, traces, profiles
- **Bottlenecks**: ranked list (owner, evidence, impact)
- **Experiments**: what to measure, expected gain, rollback
- **Changes**: code/config/schema/indexing/caching
- **Risks**: correctness, capacity, cost
- **Verification**: benchmarks, load tests, dashboards, alerts
